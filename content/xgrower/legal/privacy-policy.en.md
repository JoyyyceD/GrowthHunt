# X Grower Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GrowthHunt ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use X Grower (the "Service"). It applies to the Chrome extension, the web application at xgrower.growthhunt.ai, and any related communications.

## 1. Summary

We try to collect as little as possible. Specifically:

- 1.1 We never see your X username, password, cookies, or session tokens.
- 1.2 We never read your X timeline, your direct messages, or any post you do not actively reply to through our extension.
- 1.3 We do not sell your personal data to anyone, ever.
- 1.4 We retain reply history in truncated form (200 characters maximum) for fraud prevention and abuse audits.

## 2. What We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
When you sign up we collect your email address and, if you use Google OAuth, the basic profile fields returned by Google (display name, avatar URL, Google account ID).

### 2.2 Payment Data
When you purchase credits, our payment processor collects the payment information directly. We receive only a transaction ID, the last four digits of the card, the billing country, and the amount charged. We do not receive or store full card numbers or CVV codes.

### 2.3 Usage Data
For each AI generation or dispatch event we record:

- 2.3.1 A timestamp;
- 2.3.2 The credit amount deducted;
- 2.3.3 The first 200 characters of the inbound post you are replying to and of the draft you sent, used for abuse detection and to investigate user-reported issues;
- 2.3.4 An anonymized request ID from the LLM provider.

### 2.4 Technical Data
We collect standard technical metadata such as IP address (truncated for analytics), browser version, extension version, and error logs. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioural-tracking cookies.

### 2.5 Communications
If you email us at support@growthhunt.ai we will retain the message and your address for as long as needed to respond and to maintain a record of the support interaction.

## 3. What We Do Not Collect

- 3.1 X login credentials, cookies, or 2FA codes;
- 3.2 The content of your X DMs;
- 3.3 Your full X timeline or feed;
- 3.4 Posts that you do not select for reply through the extension;
- 3.5 Browsing activity outside the X.com domain.

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above to:

- 4.1 Provide and operate the Service (account, billing, draft generation, dispatch);
- 4.2 Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms;
- 4.3 Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries;
- 4.4 Improve product quality (aggregate, non-identifying analysis of usage patterns);
- 4.5 Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

We do not use your reply content to train any AI model.

## 5. Legal Bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases:

- 5.1 Performance of a contract: to provide the Service you have requested;
- 5.2 Legitimate interests: fraud prevention, security, product analytics in aggregate;
- 5.3 Legal obligation: tax records, response to lawful requests;
- 5.4 Consent: for any optional processing we may introduce, which you can withdraw at any time.

## 6. Subprocessors

We rely on a limited number of third-party providers to operate the Service. These subprocessors process data only on our instructions and under written agreements with confidentiality and security commitments:

- 6.1 Database and authentication hosting (Supabase);
- 6.2 Payment processing (Stripe);
- 6.3 Large language model inference (MiniMax);
- 6.4 Web and edge hosting (Vercel);
- 6.5 Transactional email delivery.

We may update this list with prospective effect. The current list is available on request.

## 7. International Transfers

Our subprocessors are located in multiple regions, and your data may be transferred outside your country of residence. Where required by law, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

## 8. Cookies

The web application uses cookies strictly necessary for authentication (your login session). We do not use third-party advertising cookies. The extension itself does not set tracking cookies on x.com or anywhere else.

## 9. Data Retention

- 9.1 Account data: kept until you delete your account, plus a short grace period for backup expiry (not more than 60 days).
- 9.2 Payment records: kept for the period required by tax law (typically 7 years).
- 9.3 Truncated reply history: kept for 12 months, then automatically purged.
- 9.4 Support emails: kept for 24 months unless a longer period is required by law.
- 9.5 Server logs: kept for up to 90 days.

## 10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (including the GDPR, the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act), you may:

- 10.1 Access the personal information we hold about you;
- 10.2 Correct inaccurate information;
- 10.3 Request deletion of your account and associated personal data;
- 10.4 Export your data in a portable format (JSON);
- 10.5 Object to or restrict certain processing;
- 10.6 Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email support@growthhunt.ai from the address associated with your account. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents have, additionally, the right to know what personal information we have collected, the right to deletion, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We do not "sell" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

## 11. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including TLS for data in transit, encryption at rest for stored credentials, principle-of-least-privilege access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure; if you suspect a security incident, please contact us immediately.

## 12. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please email us and we will delete it.

## 13. Automated Decisions

We do not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you using solely automated processing. Abuse detection signals are reviewed by a human before account-level enforcement.

## 14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-product notice at least seven (7) days before they take effect.

## 15. Contact

For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact support@growthhunt.ai.
