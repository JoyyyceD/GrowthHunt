# X Grower Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for X Grower credit purchases. It forms part of the X Grower Terms of Service and uses the same defined terms.

## 1. Summary

In short:

- 1.1 We refund unused credits only.
- 1.2 You may request one refund per account, ever.
- 1.3 Refunds are reviewed manually and processed within 5 to 7 business days of approval.
- 1.4 The full case-by-case rules are in the sections below.

## 2. What Can Be Refunded

### 2.1 Unused Credits
We refund the unused balance of credits you have purchased. Credits are deducted at the time of each AI generation or dispatch event; once a credit is consumed it cannot be refunded, because we have already paid the underlying language-model and infrastructure cost.

### 2.2 Free Trial Credits
Free trial credits are not refundable in any circumstances, as no payment was made for them.

## 3. Eligible Trigger Events

You may submit a refund request if any of the following applies:

### 3.1 14-Day No-Questions-Asked Window
You request a refund of unused credits within fourteen (14) days of the original purchase. No reason needs to be provided.

### 3.2 X Account Suspension
Your X account was suspended or permanently banned by X within thirty (30) days of your most recent credit purchase. To process this kind of request you must include a clear screenshot showing the suspension or ban notice from X and the affected handle. We may also ask you to confirm that the X account is the one you used the Service with.

### 3.3 Verified Service Defect
The Service was unavailable or materially defective for a sustained period and we have publicly acknowledged the incident. In that case the refund will, where possible, take the form of credit restoration; a cash refund of unused credits is available on request.

## 4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

The following are not eligible for a refund:

- 4.1 Credits already consumed;
- 4.2 X account suspensions occurring more than thirty (30) days after the most recent credit purchase;
- 4.3 X account suspensions where evidence cannot be provided or the screenshot cannot be verified;
- 4.4 Suspensions resulting from your violation of X's rules through conduct unrelated to X Grower (for example, content in posts you wrote and sent without using the Service);
- 4.5 Suspensions resulting from your violation of these Terms (for example, using the Service on an account you do not own, sending prohibited content, or operating multiple accounts);
- 4.6 Cases where we have reasonable evidence of fraud, chargeback abuse, or coordinated trial-credit farming;
- 4.7 Free trial credits;
- 4.8 Refund requests made more than ninety (90) days after the original purchase, except as required by mandatory consumer-protection law.

## 5. One Successful Refund Per Account

To prevent abuse, only one (1) successful refund is permitted per account over its lifetime. Subsequent refund requests will be reviewed but, except where required by law, will not be granted. Creating new accounts to obtain additional refunds is grounds for permanent termination and forfeiture of remaining credits.

## 6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

- 6.1 Send an email to support@growthhunt.ai from the address registered on your account.
- 6.2 Use the subject line: "Refund Request - [your account email]".
- 6.3 Include the order ID or approximate date of the purchase you want refunded.
- 6.4 If the request is based on an X account suspension, attach a screenshot of the suspension notice (with date and handle visible) and confirm the handle that was using the Service.
- 6.5 Tell us briefly which trigger from Section 3 applies.

We may follow up for additional information if needed to verify your request.

## 7. Review Process

Refund requests are reviewed manually. We aim to respond with an initial decision within five (5) business days, and to issue any approved refund within five (5) to seven (7) business days after approval. The actual time funds appear in your account depends on your bank and the payment processor; this may take a further ten (10) business days for international cards.

## 8. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If the original payment method is no longer valid (expired card, closed bank account), we will work with our payment processor to find an acceptable alternative; in some cases this may not be possible, and the refund will instead be issued as restored credits.

## 9. Currency and Fees

Refunds are made in the currency of the original transaction and in the same amount that you were charged, less any non-recoverable processor fees that apply where required by law. Foreign-exchange differences arising between purchase and refund are not compensated.

## 10. Account Status After Refund

After a successful refund:

- 10.1 The refunded credits are removed from your balance immediately;
- 10.2 If your remaining balance is negative or zero, your account is downgraded to free-tier status;
- 10.3 You may continue to use the Service with any remaining credits and any future purchases, subject to the Terms.

## 11. Mandatory Statutory Rights

Nothing in this Policy limits any non-waivable consumer rights you may have under the law of your country of residence (including, where applicable, statutory cooling-off periods or warranties of conformity). Where mandatory law conflicts with this Policy, the mandatory law prevails to the extent of the conflict.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time, with prospective effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. The Policy in force at the time of your purchase will govern that purchase.

## 13. Contact

All refund requests and questions should be sent to support@growthhunt.ai.
